SayeOS

Open Source Constitutional AI

An operating system for AI agents grounded in constitutional principles. Built on separation of powers, designed for small businesses, vibe coders, and everyone who shouldn't be left behind.

AI is moving from tools to autonomous agents. Without clear governance, we risk repeating history's hardest lessons: concentrated power, opacity, and unintended harm. SayeOS applies the proven framework of constitutional governance to AI - separation of powers, checks and balances, human oversight built in from the start.

The Triad Architecture

Constitutional governance through separation of concerns. Three distinct layers, each with defined authority and clear boundaries.

Intent

You decide what you want. Set goals, define boundaries, establish priorities. This is the human layer - where direction comes from.

Thought

AI reasons through options. Research, planning, trade-off analysis. All reasoning stays visible and auditable. You can question, redirect, or reject.

Action

Only approved steps execute. Nothing happens automatically. You review and approve before any action is taken in the real world.

This isn't about limiting AI. It's about building it responsibly so it amplifies human potential instead of undermining it.
